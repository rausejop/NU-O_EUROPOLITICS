# DOC01 - Concept of Operations (CONOPS)

## 1. Purpose
This document describes the leadership's intent and assumptions regarding how Nuño Europolitics platform will operate.

## 2. Background and Vision
Nuño Europolitics is aimed at providing an independent, high-level Strategic Intelligence dashboard to assess geopolitical risks in the European theater, with a special focus on supply chain impacts, full-cycle intelligence gathering, strategic forecasting, EU policy monitoring, and technical training for analysts.

## 3. Operational Environment
The system will run as a localized MVP Application utilizing a Streamlit dashboard powered by Python, utilizing SQLite as a local, self-contained data store. It guarantees privacy and self-sufficiency, functioning reliably without external cloud dependencies that might pose OPSEC risks.

## 4. Key Capabilities Envisioned
- **Geopolitical Risk Analysis**: Automated data tables evaluating regional tensions and supply constraints.
- **Full-Cycle Intelligence**: Form-driven intelligence reporting and structured database archiving.
- **Strategic Foresight**: Dashboards for visualizing 'black swan' scenarios.
- **EU Policy Monitoring**: Dedicated space for tracking EU regulatory updates.
- **Technical Capability Building**: Access to structured analysis training resources.

## 5. Security Posture
Adhering to CONF23-STD-SDLC-004, the solution emphasizes security by design complying with ISO 27001 requirements while logging every technical interaction structurally for auditing.
